## 🎨 Solution Summary

### Problem Addressed

Creative industries face significant challenges:
- Time-consuming production workflows
- Technical complexity barriers
- Limited access to advanced tools
- Difficulty translating ideas into finished outputs

### Solution Provided

Lumina Studio is an AI-powered multimodal creative platform that:
- **Accelerates ideation** through intelligent story generation
- **Simplifies visual design** with AI-generated concepts
- **Enables interactive storytelling** through scene building
- **Facilitates collaboration** with real-time AI assistance
- **Democratizes creativity** by making tools accessible
